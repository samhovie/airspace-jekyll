About Us


Hello!
We are the Bearden family and are native to Michigan where we enjoy raising our family, giving back to our community, and supporting small businesses in the area. After an almost decade-long stretch at East Jordan Iron Works, an opportunity arose and we officially established Big Bear Dock & Marine Services in early 2016.

We are currently working on expanding our services after the purchase of a new building located on US-31. Stay tuned for more updates on our showroom and outdoor display!
